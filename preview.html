Zehao Wang

I am a Ph.D. student at Concordia University in the SPEAR Lab, advised by Dr. Tse-Hsun (Peter) Chen. My current research focuses on LLM agents for software engineering, particularly their reasoning and context management when applied to real codebases. I am also broadly interested in software performance engineering and distributed systems.
